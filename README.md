## `01` About Me

<img align="right" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Anmol-Baranwal/Cool-GIFs-For-GitHub/main/e34ac646c25a2eb3ff9c04e69eb2fbc4.gif" width="300"/>

I'm a software engineer who enjoys turning ambiguous problems into dependable systems. My work sits at the intersection of **backend architecture**, **applied AI**, and **infrastructure automation** — I like building things that stay boring in production, which usually means they were designed well in the first place.

- 🔧 I design APIs and services that are easy to reason about, test, and scale
- 🤖 I build applications powered by LLMs — from prototypes to production pipelines
- ⚙️ I automate the parts of engineering that shouldn't need a human
- 📈 I care about performance, observability, and clean failure modes
- 🧩 I believe good documentation is part of the deliverable, not an afterthought

Outside of core development, I spend time reading systems-design papers, contributing to open-source tooling, and refining developer workflows that save teams hours every week.
